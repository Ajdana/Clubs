Math club

Math Club is a place to explore the beauty of mathematics beyond the classroom. Members solve challenging problems, engage in math games, and participate in competitions. It’s a space for both new and experienced math enthusiasts to improve their skills and have fun.

Debate Club

Debate Club sharpens your ability to think critically and communicate effectively. Members learn to build arguments, analyze issues from multiple perspectives, and participate in structured debates. It’s the perfect place to grow as a confident speaker and a thoughtful listener.

Financial Literacy Club

Financial Literacy Club teaches the essentials of managing money, budgeting, and investing. Members gain practical knowledge on topics like saving, credit, and financial planning. It’s an excellent resource for building skills that will last a lifetime.

Board Games Club

Board Games Club brings people together to enjoy classic and modern games in a relaxed setting. Members play a wide variety of games, from strategy and word games to collaborative adventures. It’s a great way to meet new friends and sharpen your strategic thinking.

Basketball Club

Basketball Club is for anyone who loves the sport, from beginners to seasoned players. Members practice skills, improve fitness, and enjoy friendly games and drills. It’s a dynamic, team-oriented club that promotes sportsmanship and teamwork.

Chess Club

Chess Club is a haven for anyone interested in the game, from rookies to advanced players. Members study strategies, play matches, and even participate in tournaments. It’s a perfect club for learning patience, focus, and critical thinking.

Public Speaking Club

Public Speaking Club helps members overcome the fear of speaking in front of others and develop powerful communication skills. Through speeches, workshops, and supportive feedback, members build confidence and learn effective delivery techniques. It’s a space where everyone grows as a compelling speaker.

Ping Pong Club

Ping Pong Club brings fans of table tennis together to play and improve their game. Members participate in friendly matches, learn new techniques, and enjoy a fun, energetic atmosphere. It’s a fast-paced club that enhances hand-eye coordination and agility.

Photography Club

Photography Club is a space for those interested in capturing the world through a camera lens. Members explore techniques, experiment with different styles, and go on photo walks. It’s an inspiring environment for improving skills and sharing creative ideas.
